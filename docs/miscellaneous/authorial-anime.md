| Style Category | Style Name (English) | Key Characteristics | Artists/Studios to Reference |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Prototypical Style** | **Studio Ghibli Style** | **Detailed, lush, hand-painted backgrounds; focus on nature, nostalgia, strong female protagonists, and themes of war/environmentalism.** | **Hayao Miyazaki, Isao Takahata** (*Spirited Away*, *My Neighbor Totoro*) |
| **Direct Influence** | **Studio Ponoc Style** | Highly detailed natural environments, traditional animation feel, and focus on whimsy/magic. | Studio Ponoc (*Mary and the Witch's Flower*) |
| **Whimsical/Fantasy** | **Mamoru Hosoda Style** | Emotional depth, mixing fantasy and the modern world, emphasis on family and nature. | Studio Chizu (*Wolf Children*, *Belle*) |
| **Visual Detail** | **Makoto Shinkai Style** | Hyper-realistic, vibrant lighting, and stunningly detailed backgrounds, especially of the sky and cities. | CoMix Wave Films (*Your Name*, *Weathering with You*)  |
| **Traditional Anime** | **'90s Anime Aesthetic** | Simpler character designs, often hand-drawn quality, focus on natural color palettes, and less digital polish. | Kyoto Animation (*The Melancholy of Haruhi Suzumiya*) |
| **Nature/Scenery** | **Atmospheric Anime Landscape** | Emphasis on lush forests, serene waters, and detailed architectural structures, often with a soft, diffused light. | Various (*Mushishi*, *A Letter to Momo*) |
