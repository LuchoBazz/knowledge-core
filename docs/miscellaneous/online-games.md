A quick reference of fun online games to play with friends or alone.

## Gartic Phone

[Gartic Phone](https://garticphone.com/) is a multiplayer drawing and guessing game. Each player writes a sentence, then the next player draws it, then another player describes the drawing, and so on. At the end, everyone sees the funny chain of mistakes. It works great in groups and runs directly in the browser — no download needed.

## Stopots

[Stopots](https://stopots.com/game) is a fast-paced word game based on the classic "Stop" or "Categories" game. Players choose a random letter and must fill in categories like countries, names, animals, and foods — all starting with that letter — before time runs out. It supports multiple languages and is easy to play with people online.

## Rocket Goal

[Rocket Goal](https://rocketgoal.io/) is a simple 2D physics-based soccer game. Players control a character and try to score goals against an opponent. The controls are easy to learn, but the physics make each match unpredictable and entertaining. It can be played solo against the computer or with a friend on the same device.

## Skillwarz

[Skillwarz](https://www.crazygames.com/es/game/skillwarz) is a free-to-play first-person shooter that runs in the browser. It features multiple game modes, different maps, and a variety of weapons. The game focuses on fast-paced combat and rewards players who improve their aim and movement skills over time.

## Forward Assault

[Forward Assault](https://www.crazygames.com/es/game/forward-assault) is a tactical first-person shooter with a style similar to Counter-Strike. Two teams — attackers and defenders — compete in short rounds. Players buy weapons before each round and must work together to win. It requires strategy and communication, not just fast reactions.

## Slither.io

[Slither.io](http://slither.com/io) is a browser-based multiplayer game where you control a snake and try to grow as long as possible by eating glowing dots. If your snake's head touches another player, you lose. The goal is to survive and become the biggest snake on the server. Simple rules, but very addictive.
